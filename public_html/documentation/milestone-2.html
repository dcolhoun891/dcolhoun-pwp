Milestone-2

Fullstack Cohort 34

Content Strategy:

The objective of my PWP is to present a one-page portfolio site for my wife's work as an illustrator of children's books.

The website will have the following major elements:

Header

A prominent header with my wife's business name will top the page. It will present some basic information, such as her location in Albuquerque, and a photo of her. There will be a decorative wallpaper background.

Bio

Bootstrap cards underneath the header will present a short history of her artistic work.

Portfolio

A selection of photos of her work will follow. The layout will be like a "photo album" (according to my wife). If possible, the photos, when clicked, will expand and cover a larger portion of the viewport. An effort will be made to prevent the photos from being copied or downloaded.

Contacts

Below the portfolio will be a contact form.

Footer

The footer will (at this time) mainly contain copyright information.

Wireframes

Please note: The final appearance of the web page will depend on my "client's" aesthetic and professional tastes. These wireframes are strictly tentative.
