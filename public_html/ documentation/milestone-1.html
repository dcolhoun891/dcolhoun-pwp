Duncan Colhoun's Personal Website Project

Milestone-1

Fullstack Cohort 34

Purpose:

This project's purpose is to showcase my progress training to be a fullstack web developer. It will demonstrate my skills using current front-end software. Additionally, it is an opportunity for me to practice building a professional portfolio website to advertise my wife's talents as a children's book illustrator.

Audience

The typical audience will be children's book authors looking to hire an illustrator and publishers looking to illustrate a manuscript accepted for publication. Some visitors may be librarians searching for children's titles that have a particular style of illustration.

Goals

My immediate goal is for this project to generate interviews for jobs in web development. A secondary goal is to use my pwp as a template to develop portfolio websites for my wife, her sister (a published illustrator) and various members of Albuquerque's fine arts community.
